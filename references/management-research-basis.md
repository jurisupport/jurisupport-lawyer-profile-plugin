# Management Research Basis For Lawyer Profile Strategy

Use this reference before giving a strategy audit. The point is not to decorate the audit with business words. The point is to translate established management and marketing research into questions that a serious lawyer would accept as relevant to practice positioning.

Do not overclaim. These sources are decision lenses, not proof that a specific lawyer will get more clients. If the lawyer's own evidence is missing, say so.

## Audit Standard

Every major recommendation should include:

1. Research lens: which lens justifies asking this question.
2. Lawyer-specific evidence: what record, profile text, interview answer, or source supports it.
3. Inference: the careful conclusion.
4. Action: what to change now, within 7 days, or within 30 days.
5. Limit: what is not proven yet.

## What Counts As Persuasive For Lawyers

- Specificity beats adjectives: "보증금 반환 상담 전 자료 구조화" is stronger than "민사 전문성".
- Evidence beats self-praise: records, writing patterns, consultation history, and approved explanations matter.
- Fit beats volume: a profile that attracts the wrong matter type is a business problem.
- Operating truth beats marketing ambition: do not promise response channels, speed, or matter types the office cannot sustain.
- Repairable weakness beats vague criticism: every weakness should lead to a concrete evidence, profile, marketing, or organization action.
